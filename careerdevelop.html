Career Day Program

August 18, 2025 / Auditorium

The Career Day Program connected students with professionals from various industries, providing guidance and insight into career paths.

Guest speakers delivered presentations, hosted workshops, and answered questions about education, skills, and employment opportunities.

The event inspired students to explore their interests and plan for future careers while strengthening community and professional relationships.
